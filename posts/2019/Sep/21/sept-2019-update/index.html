September 2019 Update

Published: Sat 21 September 2019
By vestapaul

In Blog.

tags: sept-2019-update

I have been lucky enough to play alongside many talented artists and musicians around Texas in 2019. Currently, I am playing with Triston Marez, an up-and-coming young artist based out of Houston, TX. We have some exciting shows on the horizon. Triston's music and most recent EP "That Was All Me" are available on all digital platforms. I also have been a sideman for my friend Logan Samford as a part of the "Neon Nights" show dates which feature both Triston and Logan. Logan's newest single, "Lovin' for the Last Time" is available on all digital platforms.
