September 2019 Update

Published: Sat 21 September 2019
By vestapaul

In Blog.

tags: sept-2019-update

I have been lucky enough to play alongside many talented artists and musicians around Texas in 2019. Currently, I am playing with Triston Marez, an up-and-coming young artist based out of Houston, TX. We have some exciting shows on the horizon. Check out his music on all digital platforms. I also I have been a sideman for my friend Logan Samford as a part of the "Neon Nights" show dates which feature Triston and Logan.
